Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is the youngest driver to ever race in Formula 1 and is the only driver to race in the sport under the age of 18. He made his F1 debut at the 2015 Australian Grand Prix at the age of 17 years and 5 months.Verstappen has won four Formula One World Drivers' Championship titles, which he won consecutively from 2021 to 2024 with Red Bull, and has won 63 Grands Prix across 10 seasons.

Sergio Perez(Checo)

Sergio 'Checo' Pérez is the most successful Mexican driver in the history of Formula One and he's achieving yet more success with Oracle Red Bull Racing.Sergio Pérez is the most successful, having won six Grands Prix.Pérez won his first championship in the national class of the 2007 British Formula 3 International Series.Sergio perez helped Max verstappen win his first ever world championship in 2021.
